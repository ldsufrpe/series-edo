E essa equação nos permite resolver o problema:

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \frac{10}{\left(1 - 10 x\right)^2} \amp = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \left(n+1\right)\left(10 x\right)^n \\\\ \amp = \sum_{n=0}^\infty 10^n\left(n+1\right)x^n \\\\ \amp = 10^0\left(0+1\right)x^0 + 10^1\left(1+1\right)x^1 + 10^2\left(2+1\right)x^2 + 10^3\left(3+1\right)x^3 + 10^4\left(4+1\right)x^4 + \cdots \\\\ \amp = 1\left(1\right)x^0 + 10\left(2\right)x^1 + 100\left(3\right)x^2 + 1000\left(4\right)x^3 + 10000\left(5\right)x^4 + \cdots \\\\ \amp = 10 x^0 + 200 x^1 + 3000 x^2 + 40000 x^3 + 500000 x^4 + \cdots \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

Assim:

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} c_0 \amp = 10 \\\\ c_1 \amp = 200 \\\\ c_2 \amp = 3000 \\\\ c_3 \amp = 40000 \\\\ c_4 \amp = 500000 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
