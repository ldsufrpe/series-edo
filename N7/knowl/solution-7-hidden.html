Para \(y=c_1\cos{3x}\) temos as derivadas:

\begin{equation*} y'=-3c_1\sin{3x} \end{equation*}

e

\begin{equation*} y'' = -9c_1\cos{3x}, \end{equation*}

assim

\begin{equation*} y''+9y = -9c_1\cos{3x}+9(c_1\cos{3x}=0. \end{equation*}

De maneira similar, para \(y=c_2\sin{3x}\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} y''+9y=-9c_2\sin{3x}+9(c_2\sin{3x}=0. \end{equation*}
