In our derivation of \(\log(1+X) = X +O(X^2)\) in Example[cross-reference to target(s) "eg_bigohlog" missing or not unique], we required only that \(|X|\le\frac{1}{2}\text{.}\) So we are free to substitute \(X= -\frac{1}{3!}x^2+O(x^4)\) for any \(x\) that is small enough that \(\big|-\frac{1}{3!}x^2+O(x^4)\big| \lt \frac{1}{2}\text{.}\)
