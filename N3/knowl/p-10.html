In the integral test, we think of a series \(\sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n\text{,}\) that we cannot evaluate explicitly, as the area of a union of rectangles, with \(a_n\) representing the area of a rectangle of width one and height \(a_n\text{.}\) Then we compare that area with the area represented by an integral, that we can evaluate explicitly, much as we did in Theorem [cross-reference to target(s) "thm_IMPcomparison" missing or not unique], the comparison test for improper integrals.
