Com \(a_n = \frac{1}{n^{9 n}}\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \sqrt[n]{a_n} = \sqrt[n]{\frac{1}{n^{9 n}}} = \frac{1}{n^{9}} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} L = \lim_{n \to \infty} \sqrt[n]{a_n} = 0 \lt 1. \end{equation*}

Portanto, a série \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{9 n}}\) converge pelo Teste da Raiz.
