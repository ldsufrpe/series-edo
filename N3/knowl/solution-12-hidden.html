Seja \(b_n = n^{9} a_n\text{.}\) Então

\begin{equation*} \rho = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left| \frac{b_{n+1}}{b_n} \right| = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left( \frac{n+1}{n} \right)^{9} \left| \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n} \right| \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = 1^{9} \cdot \frac{1}{7} = \frac{1}{7} \lt 1 \end{equation*}

Portanto, a série \(\sum\limits_{n=1}^{\infty} n^{9} a_n\) converge pelo Teste da Razão.
