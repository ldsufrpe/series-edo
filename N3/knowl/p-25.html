Now let's check the other two conditions of Theorem Teorema 1.5:

  1. Both \(x\) and \(\log x\) are positive for all \(x \gt 1\text{,}\) so \(f(x) \gt 0\) for all \(x\ge N_0=2\text{.}\)

  2. As \(x\) increases both \(x\) and \(\log x\) increase and so \(x(\log x)^p\) increases and \(f(x)\) decreases.
