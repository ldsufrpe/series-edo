Fazendo \(a_n = (-1)^n\frac{n^3}{3^n}\) temos

\begin{align*} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|=\amp \left|\frac{\dfrac{(-1)^{n+1}(n+1)^3}{3^{n+1}}}{\dfrac{(-1)^nn^3}{3^n}}\right| = \frac{(n+1)^3}{3^{n+1}}\cdot\frac{3^n}{n^3}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{3}\left(\frac{n+1}{n}\right)^3=\frac{1}{3}\left(1+ \frac{1}{n}\right)^3 \end{align*}

Logo,

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}\left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|=\frac{1}{3}\lt 1 \end{equation*}
